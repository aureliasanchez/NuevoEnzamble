Scauri Armchair

All the products in the collection communicate with each other and can be used together to furnish rooms in a prestigious, elegant manner. A huge collection of fabrics covering various styled furbish the furniture impeccably.

All the furniture has aluminium frames covered with a ribbon of hand-woven synthetic resin that creates a compact, elegant texture. These materials are especially recommended for outdoor use as they are resistant to various weather conditions, even the most difficult. There are also water-repellent fabrics used and the possibility of making the cushions totally waterproof, with an additional certified technical coating that does not allow water to enter, but which is still breathable. All the cushion covers can be removed for easy, rapid maintenance.

Customization

DFN realizes custom products suitable for every kind of outdoor environment, in order to satisfy all the client’s needs. There are no limit for the creation of a product: in fact, the company, together with a wide range of combination already inside the collection, can realize more product based on the client’s specific requests. Style, materials and dimensions are decided according to the client’s needs. An exclusive service that DFN guarantees with the purpose of answering to the requests of an expert and careful clientele.
