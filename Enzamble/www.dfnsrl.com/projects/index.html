Luxury Outdoor Projects

 

DFN, One Contractor for your entire Luxury Outdoor Project

DFN is a General Contractor specialized in luxury outdoor projects that collaborate with the designer and client as a team in the best interest of the project. A single player who coordinates the entire project, DFN is always involved in a high-level collaboration with the subcontractors that are chosen according to their qualification. Our know-how allows us to enter into the heart of projects starting right from their creation and also to intervene in the concept of outdoor areas.

We get inspiration from Italians artists, testimonials of beauty throughout the world and we want to mantain their reputation high. That’s why our projects follow the most severe quality standards and the local regulations of every country and represent unique and luxury “turnkey” projects as well as the most effective and good looking solutions.

For DFN the creation of a custom-designed project entails making sure that the finished product expresses the unique style of each customer that is faced with as many options as possible to make decisions easily. Environments created specifically for you, designed to interpret your dreams and aspirations. This is the luxury concept of DFN, a brand that has achieved a reputation for excellence all over the world.

The collaborations established over the years have allowed us to expand and apply our skills at various levels, thus offering a global outlook in every luxury outdoor project.

Our knowhow allows us to enter into the heart of projects starting right from their creation and also to intervene in the concept of outdoor areas. Our holistic approach calls for an experienced team of architects and designersto tackle the problems involved in outdoor architecture at every level:from space distribution to landscape design, from roofing to lighting and even sound diffusion, in order to create the perfect outdoor ambience. Projects that include both mobile and fixed structures such as gardens, pavements, roofs, swimming pools, etc.

We started with outdoor furnishings, then expanded our collection to include outdoor kitchens and now we have entered the sophisticated world of conservatories. Our exclusive, made-to-measure service creates customized outdoor projects of every kind, from individual pieces to an entire outdoor space. We know the style required and how to blend it with the outdoor living area, masonry, pool, and landscape, etc. This will lead to a more cohesive look for your outdoor environment.
